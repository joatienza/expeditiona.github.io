Welcome to our Places of Interest and Allergen service! 

So now you've arrived at your favorite vacation destination. Where do you go from there? With so many places to go, it's pretty easy to be overwhelmed with all the choices. Well, you can find all the places of interest around your area by following these easy steps:


  1. Enter your starting location in the origin input field.

  2. Enter your desired search radius (in miles) in the adjacent input field.

  3. Select your desired place of interest to visit.

  4. Click the Find Places button, and all the places of interest within your desired location and radius will be displayed in a list and map below!



After a long, enjoyable day of exploring and traveling, you're bound to get hungry at some point. Here at ExpeditioNA, we understand that when it comes to traveling, it is often difficultto accommodate for the needs of people with allergies. If you fall under that category, don't worry; we have you covered. Here's how you can use our food allergy feature:


  1. Click on the Allergen Info button. A box will pop up on the screen.

  2. Select your desired restaurant.

  3. Select the allergens that you wish to avoid. You may not have any, or you may have all nine. You may select as many as you need.

  4. Click the Find Foods button, and the foods that you will or will not be able to eat will be displayed on the screen!
