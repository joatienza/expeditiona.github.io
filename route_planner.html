Directions:

    Welcome to our RoutePlanner service!  

    ATTENTION: In order to operate our service, you must first install this Chrome extension here.


    With our route planning service, directions will never be a problem again. The procedure is extremely simple. All you need to do is:

    1. Enter the starting point of your journey in the 1st input field.

    2. Enter the address or name of your destination in the 2nd input field.

    3. Hit the "Create Route" button and watch the magic happen!

    4. If you want a physical copy of directions, just hit the "Export to PDF" button!
